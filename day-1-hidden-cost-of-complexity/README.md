# Day 1 – The Hidden Cost of Infrastructure Relationships

Infrastructure complexity is rarely caused by the number of resources being managed.

The real challenge comes from the relationships between resources, modules, environments, and teams.

As platforms grow, dependencies multiply, environments expand, and seemingly simple changes begin to impact multiple parts of the infrastructure.

Topics covered:

* Why infrastructure complexity grows faster than infrastructure itself
* The tradeoff between reusability and maintainability
* Module coupling and dependency management
* Terraform dependency graphs and hidden operational dependencies
* Designing Terraform for long-term change
